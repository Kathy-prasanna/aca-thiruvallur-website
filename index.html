OUR HERITAGE

More Than Four Decades of God's Faithfulness



The Apostolic Christian Assembly (ACA) is one of the most prominent indigenous Pentecostal church movements in Tamil Nadu, founded in 1948 by the late Pastor G. Sundaram, a visionary servant of God whose unwavering faith and commitment to the Gospel laid the foundation for a ministry that continues to impact countless lives.


What began as a humble fellowship of four believers in Madras (now Chennai) has, by the abundant grace of God, grown into a flourishing network of churches across Tamil Nadu, India, and beyond.


Rooted in the authority of God's Word, ACA remains steadfast in proclaiming the Gospel, making disciples, planting churches, and nurturing believers through biblical teaching, prayer, and compassionate ministry.


As part of this God-given vision, Pastor G. Sundaram appointed Pastor Paul Pandian to pioneer the ministry in Thiruvallur. Through his faithful leadership and wholehearted dedication, the church was established as a branch of the Apostolic Christian Assembly and has become a beacon of hope where countless lives have experienced the saving grace of Jesus Christ.


Today, by God's immeasurable grace and faithfulness, ACA Thiruvallur Ministries has faithfully served the community for more than forty years. Standing upon the strong spiritual foundation laid by our forefathers in faith, we continue to proclaim the hope of Jesus Christ and serve our community until He comes again.
