Our Heritage


Apostolic Christian Assembly (ACA) is one of the prominent indigenous Pentecostal church movements in Tamil Nadu, founded in 1948 by the late Pastor G. Sundaram. What began as a humble fellowship has grown into a flourishing ministry with churches serving communities across Tamil Nadu and beyond.


ACA Thiruvallur Ministries was established under the leadership of Pastor M. Paul Pandian. Through God's faithfulness, the church has served the people of Thiruvallur for more than four decades through biblical preaching, worship, discipleship, prayer and evangelism.

Our Mission

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making faithful disciples, strengthening families, equipping believers through God's Word, and serving our community with Christ-like love.

Our Vision


To be a Christ-centred church that transforms lives through the power of the Holy Spirit, raises spiritually mature believers, plants churches, and reaches every generation with the hope of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

What We Believe

We believe in the authority of the Holy Bible, salvation through Jesus Christ, the power of the Holy Spirit, the importance of prayer, biblical discipleship, holy living, and proclaiming the Gospel until Christ returns.
