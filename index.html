Our Heritage


Apostolic Christian Assembly (ACA) is one of the prominent indigenous Pentecostal church movements in Tamil Nadu, founded in 1948 by the late Pastor G. Sundaram. What began as a humble fellowship has grown into a flourishing ministry with churches serving communities across Tamil Nadu and beyond.


ACA Thiruvallur Ministries was established under the leadership of Pastor M. Paul Pandian. Through God's faithfulness, the church has been serving the people of Thiruvallur for over four decades with biblical teaching, prayer, worship, evangelism, and compassionate ministry.

Our Mission

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making faithful disciples, strengthening families, equipping believers through the Word of God, and serving our community with Christ-like love.

Our Vision


To be a Christ-centred church that transforms lives through the power of the Holy Spirit, raises spiritually mature believers, plants churches, and reaches every generation with the hope of the Gospel.

What We Believe

The Holy Bible

We believe that the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, our Lord and Saviour, who died and rose again for the salvation of mankind.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the ministry and gifts of the Holy Spirit, who empowers believers to live holy lives and faithfully serve God.
