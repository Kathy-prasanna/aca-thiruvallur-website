Our Heritage


The Apostolic Christian Assembly (ACA) is one of the most prominent indigenous Pentecostal church movements in Tamil Nadu, founded in 1948 by the late Pastor G. Sundaram. What began as a humble fellowship of four believers in Madras (now Chennai) has, by the abundant grace of God, grown into a flourishing network of churches across Tamil Nadu, India, and beyond.


Rooted firmly in the authority of God's Word, ACA remains committed to proclaiming the Gospel, making disciples, planting churches, and nurturing believers through biblical teaching, prayer, and compassionate ministry.


Pastor G. Sundaram prayerfully appointed Pastor M. Paul Pandian to establish the ministry in Thiruvallur. Through God's faithfulness and his dedicated leadership, the church has faithfully served the community for over four decades.

Our Mission

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making faithful disciples, strengthening families, equipping believers through the Word of God, and serving our community with Christ-like love.

Our Vision


To be a Christ-centred church that transforms lives through the power of the Holy Spirit, raises spiritually mature believers, plants churches, and reaches every generation with the hope of the Gospel.

What We Believe

The Holy Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, our Lord and Saviour, who died and rose again for our salvation.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the ministry and gifts of the Holy Spirit, who empowers believers to live holy lives and serve God faithfully.
