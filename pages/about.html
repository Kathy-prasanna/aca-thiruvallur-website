OUR HERITAGE

More Than Four Decades of God's Faithfulness



The Apostolic Christian Assembly (ACA) is one of the most prominent indigenous Pentecostal church movements in Tamil Nadu. Founded in 1948 by the late Pastor G. Sundaram, the ministry began with only four believers and has now grown into a network of churches across Tamil Nadu and beyond.


Pastor M. Paul Pandian faithfully established the Thiruvallur branch under God's guidance. Through decades of prayer, evangelism, worship, discipleship, and compassionate ministry, ACA Thiruvallur continues to serve the community with the love of Christ.

Our Mission

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making faithful disciples, strengthening families, equipping believers through the Word of God, and serving our community with Christ-like love.

OUR VISION

Looking Towards the Future


Our vision is to be a Christ-centred church that transforms lives through the power of the Holy Spirit, raises spiritually mature believers, plants churches, and reaches every generation with the hope of the Gospel.

What We Believe

Our faith is firmly rooted in the timeless truths of God's Holy Word.

The Holy Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, serving as the final authority for faith and life.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, our Lord and Saviour, who died on the cross, rose again, and offers eternal life to everyone who believes in Him.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the ministry and gifts of the Holy Spirit, who empowers believers to live holy lives, serve faithfully, and boldly proclaim the Gospel.
