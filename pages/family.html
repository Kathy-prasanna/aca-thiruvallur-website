FELLOWSHIP MINISTRY

Family Fellowship

Growing Families in Christ

At Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur Ministries, we believe that Christ-centred families build a Christ-centred church. Our monthly Family Fellowship strengthens marriages, nurtures children, and encourages every home to grow together in faith.

Through worship, biblical teaching, prayer, and fellowship, families are equipped to face life's challenges with God's wisdom and grace.

OUR MINISTRY

Building Strong Christian Families



The Family Fellowship is led by Pastor M. Paul Pandian, who faithfully shares biblical wisdom and practical teaching to help families build strong, Christ-centred homes.


Each gathering includes worship, prayer, Bible teaching, fellowship, and discussions that encourage husbands, wives, parents, and children to grow together spiritually.


Families are strengthened through God's Word as they learn biblical principles for marriage, parenting, forgiveness, unity, and living as faithful witnesses for Christ.


Whether you are newly married, raising children, or enjoying later years together, our Family Fellowship provides a welcoming place to grow in faith alongside other Christian families.
