Family Fellowship

At the Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur, we believe that strong families are built upon a strong foundation in Christ. Our Family Fellowship is conducted once every month on the second Sunday, providing a dedicated time for married couples and families to grow together in faith, love, and fellowship.

Together, we seek to strengthen our homes through God's Word and encourage one another to live Christ-centred lives.

Growing Families in Christ

The fellowship is led by Pastor Paul Pandian, who shares biblical wisdom, practical counsel, and Christ-centred principles for building a God-fearing home.


Through Scripture-based teaching, interactive discussions, and pastoral guidance, couples are encouraged to strengthen their marriages, nurture healthy family relationships, and face life's challenges with faith, wisdom, and love.


Over the years, this ministry has become a source of encouragement and transformation for many families. Countless couples have testified to God's faithfulness as they experienced renewed unity, spiritual growth, and deeper relationships through the truths shared during these gatherings.


Whether you are newly married or have been married for many years, we warmly invite you to become a part of this enriching fellowship. You will be welcomed with love by our church family as we grow together in God's grace and purpose for our homes.
