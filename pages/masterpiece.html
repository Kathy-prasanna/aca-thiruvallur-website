SUNDAY SCHOOL MINISTRY

Masterpiece Bible School

Building Young Lives on God's Word

At Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur Ministries, we believe that every child is uniquely created by God with a divine purpose. Masterpiece Bible School is dedicated to nurturing children and teenagers in the knowledge, love, and truth of God's Word.

Our mission is to help every child grow spiritually, develop Christ-like character, and build a lifelong relationship with Jesus Christ.

OUR MINISTRY

Learning God's Word with Joy



Every Sunday, our dedicated teachers guide children through engaging Bible lessons that help them understand God's Word in a joyful and meaningful way.


Each class includes worship songs, memory verses, interactive games, storytelling, prayer, and age-appropriate Bible teaching to help every child grow spiritually.


Lessons are carefully planned to help children understand biblical truths, apply them in everyday life, and develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.


Throughout the year, students participate in Scripture memory competitions, assessments, special programs, and celebrations that encourage both spiritual and personal growth.


Our prayer is that every child who attends Masterpiece Bible School will become a faithful disciple of Christ and a future leader in God's Kingdom.

"Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it."

Proverbs 22:6
