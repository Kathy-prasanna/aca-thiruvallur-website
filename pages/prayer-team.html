Prayer Team

Standing in the Gap Through Prayer

Prayer is the foundation of every ministry at Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur. Our Prayer Team faithfully intercedes for the church, its ministries, families, and the community, believing that God hears and answers the prayers of His people.

OUR MINISTRY

A Ministry Built on Prayer



The Prayer Team is the spiritual backbone of Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur. Rooted in the conviction that prayer is the foundation of every fruitful ministry, the church is blessed with several dedicated prayer groups that faithfully intercede for the congregation, its ministries, the community, and the advancement of God's Kingdom.


Comprising both men and women who are deeply committed to seeking the Lord, the Prayer Team gathers regularly to pray with faith, unity, and perseverance. We believe that every testimony, every soul reached, and every step of growth our church has experienced is a result of God's grace working through the prayers of His people.


Guided by the biblical truth that "The prayer of a righteous person is powerful and effective." (James 5:16), the team continues to stand in the gap, trusting God to accomplish His perfect will and bring glory to His name.

We Pray For

The Church • Families • Missions • Evangelism • Healing • Youth • Children • Community • Revival • God's Kingdom

OUR PURPOSE

Seeking God Together



Prayer is not merely a ministry we participate in—it is the heartbeat of our church. Through united prayer, we seek God's wisdom, direction, strength, and blessing for every aspect of church life. Our greatest desire is to see lives transformed, families strengthened, and God's name glorified through the power of prayer.

"The prayer of a righteous person is powerful and effective."

James 5:16
