YOUTH FELLOWSHIP

Potter's House

Potter's House is the vibrant youth fellowship of the Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur—a place where young people come together to worship, grow, and glorify the name of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.

More than just a gathering, Potter's House is a Christ-centred community where young hearts discover their God-given purpose, develop their gifts, and become faithful disciples of Christ.

A Place to Grow

One of the unique aspects of Potter's House is its commitment to identifying and nurturing the hidden talents and abilities of young people, both within our church family and from the wider community.


Through opportunities to serve, lead, worship, and participate in various activities, every young person is encouraged to use their God-given potential for the glory of God.


The fellowship is led by Pastor Jabez Philip, Assistant Pastor and Youth Leader of ACA Thiruvallur. Through sound biblical teaching, practical life lessons, and relatable examples, he equips young people to stand firm in Christ and live faithfully in today's world.

"Let no one despise your youth, but set the believers an example in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith, in purity."

1 Timothy 4:12
