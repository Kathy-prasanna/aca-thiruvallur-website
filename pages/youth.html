YOUTH FELLOWSHIP

Potter's House

Growing Together in Christ

Potter's House is the vibrant youth fellowship of Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur, where young people gather to worship God, build lasting friendships, and grow deeper in their walk with Jesus Christ.

More than just a meeting, Potter's House is a Christ-centered community where young hearts discover their God-given purpose, develop their gifts, and become faithful disciples of Christ.

YOUTH MINISTRY

A Place to Grow



One of the unique aspects of Potter's House is its commitment to identifying, nurturing, and developing the gifts and talents that God has placed within every young person.


Through worship, Bible teaching, discussions, outreach activities, leadership opportunities, and fellowship, every participant is encouraged to grow spiritually while serving Christ with excellence.


The fellowship is led by Pastor Jabez Philip, Assistant Pastor and Youth Leader of ACA Thiruvallur. Through biblical teaching, practical life lessons, and personal mentoring, he encourages young people to live boldly for Christ and become faithful witnesses in today's world.

"Let no one despise your youth, but set the believers an example in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith, and in purity."

1 Timothy 4:12

WHAT WE DO

What Happens at Potter's House?



Praise & Worship

A joyful time of worship where young people unite to glorify God through songs and prayer.

Bible Teaching

Practical and Christ-centered messages that help young people apply God's Word in their daily lives.

Games & Fellowship

Interactive activities, discussions, and fellowship that build friendships and strengthen unity.

Talent Development

Young people are encouraged to discover and use their God-given gifts through music, speaking, media, worship, and ministry.
