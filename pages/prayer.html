Share Your Prayer Request



At Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur Ministries, we firmly believe that God hears every sincere prayer. No burden is too great, and no request is too small for our loving Heavenly Father.


Whether you are praying for healing, peace, wisdom, family, education, employment, financial provision, or spiritual growth, our pastoral team would be honoured to stand with you in prayer.


Every prayer request is treated with care, compassion, and complete confidentiality. We believe that God is faithful and continues to work powerfully in the lives of those who seek Him.
