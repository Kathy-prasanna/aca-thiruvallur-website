Media Team

Using Technology to Proclaim Christ

The Media Team of Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur is committed to using technology and creativity to advance the Gospel and support every ministry of the church. Through dedication, innovation, and servant-hearted leadership, the team works behind the scenes to ensure that every service and event reaches people with excellence.

OUR MINISTRY

Serving God Through Media



The Media Team of Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur is committed to using technology and creativity to advance the Gospel and support the ministry of the church. Managed by a dedicated group of young professionals and tech-driven youth, the team faithfully serves behind the scenes, ensuring that every service, event, and ministry is effectively communicated both within the church and to the wider community.


From live streaming and photography to videography, graphic design, social media, website management, and multimedia production, the Media Team combines technical excellence with a heart for ministry. Every effort is dedicated to glorifying God, strengthening the church's outreach, and proclaiming the message of Jesus Christ through modern technology.

Areas of Ministry

Photography • Videography • Live Streaming • Graphic Design • Website Management • Social Media • Multimedia Production

OUR PURPOSE

Serving Behind the Scenes



While much of our work happens away from the spotlight, every photograph captured, every livestream broadcast, every graphic designed, and every video produced is done with one purpose—to glorify God and make the Gospel accessible to people everywhere. We believe that modern technology can be a powerful tool for advancing God's Kingdom when used faithfully and with excellence.

"Declare His glory among the nations, His marvelous deeds among all peoples."

Psalm 96:3

Be Part of the Media Team

If you have a passion for photography, videography, design, technology, editing, or media production and desire to use your gifts for God's Kingdom, we warmly invite you to serve alongside us.
