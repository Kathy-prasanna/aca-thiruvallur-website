OUR SHEPHERDS

Pastoral Team

Faithfully Shepherding God's People

The Pastoral Team of Apostolic Christian Assembly is committed to faithfully preaching God's Word, shepherding His people, and equipping believers to grow in Christ. United in vision and purpose, our pastors serve with humility, compassion, and unwavering dedication to the Kingdom of God.

OUR LEADERS

Meet Our Pastoral Team

Pr. Paul Pandian

Senior Pastor

Pastor Paul Pandian is the Senior Pastor of Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur. A faithful and obedient servant of God, he leads the congregation with humility, wisdom, and unwavering commitment to the truth of God's Word. Through his shepherding ministry, he encourages believers to grow in Christ, walk in holiness, and fulfill God's purpose for their lives.

Pr. Jabez Philip

Assistant Pastor & Youth Pastor

Pastor Jabez Philip serves as the Assistant Pastor and Youth Pastor with a deep passion for mentoring the younger generation. Through biblical teaching, discipleship, and compassionate leadership, he inspires young people to develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ and become faithful followers of Him.

Pr. Stalin

Pastor

Pastor Stalin is a devoted servant of God who ministers with humility and wholehearted dedication. Blessed with a passion for worship, he glorifies God through the gift of music, leading others into heartfelt praise and worship. His ministry reflects a life surrendered to Christ, inspiring believers to worship God in spirit and in truth while faithfully serving His Kingdom.

Pr. Caleb Karthick

Pastor Caleb Karthick faithfully oversees the ministry in Thozhuvur with a heart committed to serving God's people. Pr. Paramanandham

Pastor — Ikkadu Ministries

Pr. Michael Rajan
