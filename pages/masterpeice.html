SUNDAY SCHOOL MINISTRY

Masterpiece Bible School

At the Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur, we believe that every child and teenager is a masterpiece created by God, with a unique purpose and calling.

Masterpiece Bible School is our dedicated Sunday School ministry, committed to laying a strong biblical foundation in the lives of children and young people from an early age.

Learning God's Word with Joy

Every Sunday, our devoted and passionate teachers lead engaging sessions designed to help children grow in the knowledge and love of God's Word.


Each class includes joyful worship through songs, interactive games, memory verses, and age-appropriate Bible lessons that make learning meaningful, enjoyable, and life-changing.


Lessons are systematically taught from carefully selected books of the Bible, helping every student gain a deeper understanding of Scripture while developing a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.


Children are periodically provided with coaching and revision sessions before assessments based on the portions they have studied. Students who perform well are honoured with special prizes, while every participant receives appreciation and encouragement for their effort.

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders

Masterpiece Bible School is more than a classroom—it is a place where children grow spiritually, build lasting friendships, discover their gifts, and experience the transforming love of Jesus Christ.

We warmly welcome every child and teenager to become a part of this exciting journey of faith, where lives are shaped by God's Word and prepared to become tomorrow's Christ-centred leaders.
