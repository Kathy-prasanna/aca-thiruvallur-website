Music Team

Leading Hearts into God's Presence

The Music Team of Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur is dedicated to leading the congregation into the presence of God through Christ-centered worship. Every song we sing and every instrument we play is offered as an act of worship, lifting high the name of Jesus Christ.

OUR MINISTRY

Worship with Excellence



The Music Team of Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur is dedicated to leading the congregation into the presence of God through Christ-centered worship. Comprising talented keyboardists, guitarists, saxophonists, pad players, and a devoted choir, the team serves with excellence, unity, and a heart of worship, using their God-given gifts to glorify the Lord alone.


Under the leadership of Pr. Jabez Philip and Sis. Arelene Janice, the ministry is committed to cultivating both spiritual devotion and musical excellence. Every song and every instrument is offered as an act of worship, creating an atmosphere where believers can encounter God's presence and lift His name in praise. Together, the team strives to lead the church in worship that is biblical, Spirit-led, and centered on the glory of Jesus Christ.

Our Worship Ministry

Keyboardists • Guitarists • Saxophonists • Pad Players • Choir • Worship Leaders

OUR PURPOSE

Worship that Glorifies Christ



True worship is more than music—it is the heartfelt response of believers to the greatness of God. Our Music Team seeks to create an atmosphere where the church can worship in spirit and in truth, focusing every heart on Jesus Christ. Through faithful preparation, unity, and dependence on the Holy Spirit, we desire to lead God's people into genuine worship that brings glory to His name.

"Sing to the Lord a new song; sing to the Lord, all the earth."

Psalm 96:1

Serve Through Worship

If God has blessed you with musical gifts and a heart to worship Him, we invite you to become part of the Music Team. Together, let us use our talents to glorify Christ and lead His people in worship.
