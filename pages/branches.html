EXPANDING GOD'S KINGDOM

Our Branch Churches

One Vision • Many Congregations

Throughout the Scriptures, God faithfully raises servants to continue His work from one generation to the next. By His abundant grace, ACA Thiruvallur Ministries has grown into several branch churches that faithfully proclaim the Gospel and serve their local communities.

Each congregation stands as a testimony to God's faithfulness, bringing hope, biblical teaching, worship, and discipleship to people across different regions.

OUR MINISTRIES

Our Branch Churches



Palavakkam Ministries

Pastor Michael Rajan

The first branch church established through God's abundant grace. Pastor Michael Rajan, formerly an Associate Pastor at ACA Thiruvallur, faithfully shepherds this congregation through biblical preaching, compassionate pastoral care, and a heart for evangelism.

Thozhuvur Ministries

Pastor Caleb Karthik

Led by Pastor Caleb Karthik, this congregation continues to grow through Christ-centred preaching, discipleship, prayer, and faithful pastoral ministry. The church remains committed to sharing the Gospel and strengthening believers.

Ikkadu Ministries

Pastor Paramanandham

Pastor Paramanandham faithfully leads the Ikkadu branch through worship, evangelism, biblical teaching, and compassionate ministry. God continues to use this church to bring many people to the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ.
