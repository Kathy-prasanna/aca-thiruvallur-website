OUR MINISTRIES

Branch Churches

"As the Lord was with Moses, so He was with Joshua." Throughout the Scriptures, God faithfully raises servants to continue His work from one generation to the next. Through His abundant grace and mercy, the Apostolic Christian Assembly, Thiruvallur has grown beyond one congregation into several thriving branch churches.

Each church stands as a testimony of God's faithfulness and His desire that every community would hear the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Branch Churches

Palavakkam Ministries

The first branch church established through the immeasurable grace of God is located in Palavakkam. This congregation is led by Pastor Michael Rajan, who faithfully served as an Associate Pastor at ACA Thiruvallur.

His humble leadership, biblical preaching, compassionate heart and dedication to God's Kingdom continue to strengthen the believers while reaching the surrounding community with the Gospel.

Thozhuvur Ministries

This congregation is shepherded by Pastor Caleb Karthik, another former Associate Pastor of ACA Thiruvallur.

Through Christ-centred preaching, biblical guidance and pastoral care, the church continues to witness God's transforming power in many lives.

Ikkadu Ministries

The Ikkadu branch church is led by Pastor Paramanandham, a gifted pastor, worship leader and evangelist.

God continues to use his ministry through powerful preaching, heartfelt worship and evangelistic outreach, bringing many people to experience the saving grace of Jesus Christ.

To God Be The Glory

Every branch church stands as a testimony to God's unfailing faithfulness and sovereign guidance. We remain grateful that the Lord has raised faithful shepherds who continue to lead His people with biblical conviction, servant-hearted love and unwavering dedication. May every ministry continue to proclaim Christ until He comes.
